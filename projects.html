Multi-Skill Development Centre (MSDC) Jalandhar

A showcase of state-of-the-art government institutional infrastructure and civil engineering precision.

Project Overview

Project Overview: The Multi-Skill Development Centre (MSDC) in Jalandhar, Punjab, is a marquee civic infrastructure project executed successfully by Harjeet Singh Contractor for the Government of Punjab under the Punjab Skill Development Mission (PSDM). Designed to empower the youth of Punjab with industry-aligned technical skills, this expansive institutional facility was constructed adhering to strict government deadlines and rigorous quality benchmarks. The project involved multi-phase civil construction, including the core academic block, advanced engineering and manufacturing labs, administrative infrastructure, and comprehensive site development featuring modern exterior grading, a dynamic structural ramp network, and robust perimeter security walls.

Client: Punjab Skill Development Mission (PSDM), Government of Punjab
Location: Jalandhar, Punjab
Project Status: Completed (Class-I Contract Execution)
Engineering Scope: Academic Blocks, Engineering Labs, Structural Ramps
