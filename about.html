Engineering Excellence & Compliance

Founded as a sole proprietorship, Harjeet Singh Contractor has established a formidable reputation for delivering heavy civil infrastructure. We specialize in civil construction, structural works, and corporate civic structures.

Our operation is built on deep structural engineering expertise, meticulous compliance with public works guidelines, and strict adherence to government deadlines and budgets. As a PWD-registered Class-I contractor, we participate in complex civic bidding and execute municipal-level structural updates.
